Background

Hello! My name is Dorian Tykesson, and I am an Interaction Designer with a passion for optimising user experiences. My foundation in cognitive science and psychology, combined with my curiosity about the human mind and our interaction with physical and digital artefacts, has led me to pursue a career in this field.

My academic journey began in California, where I discovered my passion for cognitive science through its various interdisciplinary fields. With an associate's degree in psychology from Miami, I returned to Sweden. Back in Gothenburg, I earned my Bachelor's in Cognitive Science and will graduate with my Master's in Interaction Design & Technologies program from Chalmers in June 2024.

My academic undertakings are a testament to my eagerness to grow within the field. I am particularly interested in exploring the vast potential of interaction design to positively influencing mental health and well-being. However, my curiosity extends beyond this area, and I am excited about applying interaction design across various domains and seeking opportunities to innovate and make meaningful contributions wherever needed.

As I embark on my professional journey, I desire to create positive change through design. My unique blend of worldly experience, skills, and insights, acquired through my education and travels, has equipped me with a solid foundation for a career dedicated to enhancing user experiences and tackling challenges with empathy and creativity.
